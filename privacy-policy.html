Privacy Policy

Kara Job Updates  ·  KuvukilandBot  ·  Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Kara Job Updates is a free Facebook Page and automated bot (KuvukilandBot) that shares job opportunities, learnerships, internships, and bursaries for unemployed youth in South Africa. We are not a recruitment agency and do not charge any fees.

2. What Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from visitors or followers. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or ID number.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels on this page.
  • We do not run any forms that capture user data.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

3. How the Bot Works

KuvukilandBot is an automated script that reads publicly available job listing websites (RSS feeds and public web pages) and reposts summaries to the Kara Job Updates Facebook Page using the Facebook Graph API.

The bot only reads public job listing data — it does not interact with, read, or store any information about Facebook users or page followers.

4. Facebook Data

This app uses the Facebook Graph API solely to publish posts to the Kara Job Updates Facebook Page. We use the following permission:

  • pages_manage_posts — to publish job listing posts to our own page.

We do not request access to any user's personal Facebook data, friends list, messages, or any other user information.

Facebook's own privacy policy applies to all interactions on their platform: facebook.com/policy.php

5. Third-Party Links

Posts on our page contain links to third-party job listing websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. Always review their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our page is intended for job seekers aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 18.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.
